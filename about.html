How to Get Started

  • Visit a local bookstore or library to explore manga titles.
  • Choose a genre that interests you.
  • Start with popular series such as Naruto, One Piece, or Attack on Titan.
  • Read online manga platforms like MangaDex or Crunchyroll.
  • Create a reading schedule to balance manga and schoolwork.
  • Join online manga communities and forums to connect with other manga enthusiasts.
  • Purchase physical manga volumes or subscribe to manga magazines.
